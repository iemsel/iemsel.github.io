Personal SWOT Analysis

A SWOT analysis is a tool used to assess a business, project, or individual by looking at four key areas: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. A SWOT analysis helps you understand what you're doing well, what needs improvement, what opportunities you can pursue, and what risks to watch out for.
